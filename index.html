ABOUT

Enjoy concert night at your hometown

Welcome to Slytherin! We are a team of experienced professionals who are passionate about bringing the best live music experiences to our audiences. At our company, we believe that music has the power to bring people together and create unforgettable memories. That's why we work tirelessly to ensure that every concert we organize is a success, from the selection of artists to the final note of the performance. Our team consists of individuals with diverse backgrounds and skillsets, including event planning, marketing, technical support, and artist management. With our combined expertise, we are able to deliver high-quality concerts that meet the unique needs and preferences of our clients and audiences.

Music

A selection of most popular songs

Jai Jai Shivshankar



"Jai Jai Shivshankar" from War in the voice of Vishal Dadlani, Benny Dayal featuring Hrithik Roshan and Tiger Shroff. The music of new song is given by Vishal and Shekhar while Lyrics penned by Kumaar and Video directed by Siddharth Anand.


Brown Munde



"Brown Munde" meaning is all the men from the Indian Sub-Continent, also termed as Desi. This term became viral after the song “Brown Munde” by AP Dhillon.


Raabta



"Raabta" is a beautiful love song that has become a popular favorite among Hindi music lovers. It is a testament to the enduring power of love and the beauty of human connection.


Born To Shine



The song is an inspirational anthem that encourages listeners to pursue their dreams and overcome obstacles to achieve success. It is a celebration of individuality, hard work, and determination, and has become a popular favorite among Punjabi music lovers. Overall, "Born to Shine" is an uplifting and motivating song that inspires listeners to reach for the stars and never give up on their dreams.
